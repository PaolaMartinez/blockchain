 Resources Information Information A blockchain is a growing list of records, called blocks, which are linked using cryptography. Each block contains a cryptographic hash of the previous block, a timestamp, and transaction data. It is an open and distributed ledger that record transactions between two parties efficiently, and in a verifiable and permanent way. In the blockchain, a proof of work is used to confirm transactions and to produce new blocks in the chain. A proof of work is a piece of data which satisfies certain requirements, that is difficult to produce, but easy for others to verify.
